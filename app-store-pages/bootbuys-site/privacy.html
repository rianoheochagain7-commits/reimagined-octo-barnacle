BootBuys Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-08-18

Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email, profile info you choose to add.
  • Content: listings, photos, messages you send within the app.
  • Diagnostics & analytics: app usage, performance, and crash data (via Apple and Firebase).
  • Device data: device model, OS version, anonymized identifiers for analytics.

How we use information

  • Provide core features (browse, search, listings, messaging, notifications).
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and enhance safety (fraud and abuse prevention).
  • Communicate service updates and respond to support requests.

Sharing and third parties

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We use service providers to help operate BootBuys (e.g., Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics) under contracts that limit their use of data to services for us.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect users and our services.

Data retention

We retain information while your account is active and as needed to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion at any time.

Your choices & rights

  • Update or delete your profile information in the app.
  • Request data deletion or access by emailing BootBuys@gmail.com.
  • Control notifications in Settings on your device and within the app.

Children's privacy

BootBuys is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

International users

Data may be processed in the country where you live and other countries where our providers operate. Protections are applied consistently.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted here with a new effective date.
